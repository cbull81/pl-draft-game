TACTICOS & GALACTICOS · A FOOTBALL DRAFT GAME
What is Tacticos & Galacticos?
A blind football draft game inspired by 82-0.com. Each round rolls a random club and season — you pick one player from that squad to fill a slot in your locked formation. Once your XI is complete, a model predicts how many Premier League points that team would earn over a 38-game season.
How does scoring work?
Your XI is scored on a predicted-points model trained on real team seasons across the top European leagues. Attacking output comes from your players' individual xG/xA stats; defensive quality is estimated from their (position- and season-normalised) market values. The final score is clipped to the 0–114 range — 114 being a perfect 38-win season.
What is "blind draft" mode?
Player stats, market values, and caps are hidden during selection — you only see names and positions. Use your football knowledge to pick well. Everything is revealed on the final results screen.
What do rerolls do?
Each game starts with a small number of rerolls. Spending one skips the current club-season and rolls a new one — useful if the rolled squad has no eligible players for your open slots, or if it's a particularly thin promoted side.
What is World Cup mode?
Instead of club seasons, each round rolls one of the 48 nations competing at the 2026 FIFA World Cup. Squads are sourced from the actual 2026 tournament rosters. Scoring is a pedigree index based on market value, international caps, and international goals — no xG data is used since club-level stats don't map cleanly to international football. There are no rerolls in WC mode.
What do the World Cup tiers mean?
Tiers are set from the distribution of optimal XIs across all 48 nations:

🏆 World Cup Champions — top-tier squad, real contender
🥈 Finalists — elite but not quite the best
🥉 Semi-finalists — strong tournament run expected
💜 Quarter-finalists — solid squad, could go far
⚽ Round of 16 — decent but limited ceiling
❌ Group Stage Exit — struggles to compete at this level
Where does the data come from?
Understat — xG and xA per player, per season (2014/15 onwards).
Transfermarkt via the dcaribou open dataset — market valuations, international caps, clean positions, roster data.
Wikipedia — 2026 World Cup squad lists.

This is an independent project and is not affiliated with the Premier League, FIFA, Understat, or Transfermarkt. Market values are crowd-sourced estimates, not transfer fees.
Why does my score seem low even with good players?
The model rewards balance — a great attack paired with a weak defence will be punished on the predicted-points side. Defensive quality is estimated from market value, so budget centre-backs will drag the score down even if your forwards are world-class.
